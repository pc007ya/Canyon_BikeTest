步驟 04Step 04

照 7 個步驟製作，上傳實拍對比官方成品圖Follow the 7 steps; upload your photo beside the official one

1
烘烤麵包The Toast
三片麵包切面朝下，烘至金黃酥脆。Toast all three buns cut-side down until crisp.
2
醬汁與底層鋪料The Foundation
底層＋中層各塗特製醬、洋蔥、生菜、起司／酸黃瓜。Sauce, onion, lettuce, cheese/pickles on both buns.
3
高溫煎製牛肉排The Sear
大火壓煎 10 秒鎖汁，兩面各煎約 1 分鐘。Press-sear 10s, ~1 min per side.
4
黃金交疊組裝 IBuilding Up I
兩片肉排分別放上底層與中層生菜。Place each patty on its lettuce layer.
5
黃金交疊組裝 IIBuilding Up II
中層疊上底層，蓋上頂層芝麻麵包，完成七層。Stack middle on bottom, crown with sesame bun.
6
包裝檢驗Quality Check
裝盒後檢查筆直、七層分明、無外溢。Boxed: upright, 7 layers, no spillover.
7
封盒出餐Clamshell for Serving
雙手扣緊掀蓋盒，鎖住熱氣與風味，出餐。Snap-lock the clamshell to trap steam & flavor, then serve.

步驟 07Step 07

廚具怎麼查How to look up a tool

「設備盤點」 頁列出每項廚具用在哪些測試。需求為所有測試的 最高單次用量（廚具共用、不累加）。The Equipment page lists which tests use each tool. Demand is the peak per-test usage (shared, not summed).

同一個煎台可以做很多測試，所以算「最高」不是「相加」。One griddle serves many tests — so it's the "max", not a sum.

十步走完，用大麥克讀懂整套系統Ten steps — the whole system, via one Big Mac

登入 → 目前專案：DEMO / 密碼 DEMO，只看到被分配的 BigMac。Login → Projects: DEMO / code DEMO, see only the assigned BigMac.
食材＋廚具 → 製作：6 食材、3 廚具，照 7 步驟做，上傳實拍對比官圖。Food + tools → cook: 6 ingredients, 3 tools, 7 steps, upload a photo vs the official one.
